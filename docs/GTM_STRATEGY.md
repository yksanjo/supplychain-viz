## üö® Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Slow Sales Cycle
**Mitigation:**
- Pilot program to accelerate evaluation
- ROI guarantee to reduce risk
- Case studies from similar companies

### Risk: Integration Complexity
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on NetSuite first (easiest)
- Professional services for complex setups
- Clear implementation timeline guarantees

### Risk: Competitor Response
**Mitigation:**
- Speed to market
- AI differentiation (hard to replicate quickly)
- Customer lock-in through data/network effects

### Risk: Market Timing
**Mitigation:**
- Supply chain visibility is trending post-COVID
- Nearshoring = more complex supply chains
- Economic uncertainty = need for optimization
